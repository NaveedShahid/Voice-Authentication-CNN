What is this Website for?


SaiFETY is a project extending from the video compression pipeline Txt2Vid (see below).

Specifically, we are testing new safety methods to protect user privacy as AI and deepfakes become more and more prevelant.

The sentences we ask users to record have very high phoenetic coverage, meaning they use many unique sounds in the english language.

For more information on Text2Vid, please feel free to look thourough the GitHub below:

Txt2Vid Information/GitHub
